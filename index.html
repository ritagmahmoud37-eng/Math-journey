Session Structure

  1. Reviewing homework and correcting mistakes.
  2. Explaining questions the student did not understand.
  3. Explaining the new lesson.
  4. Solving problems together during the session.
  5. Leaving some questions for the student to solve independently during the session.
  6. Assigning homework for practice.
